Day 02
Day 2
Tianmen Mountain & Tujia Folk Customs Park
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Tianmen Mountain Scenic Area (cable car up) → West Line (Glass Skywalk, Guigu Plank Road, Tianmen Temple) → Summit cable car → Yunmeng Pavilion → East Line → Mountain‑penetrating escalator → Tianmen Cave → Descend 999 steps → Cable car down → Lunch → Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi City) → Dayong Ancient City → Overnight at Zhangjiajie International Hotel → Optional evening show: “Tianmen Fox Fairy”
✈ TransportPrivate car, cable car
🏨 HotelZhangjiajie International Hotel
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Tianmen Mountain Cable Car — One of the world’s longest rides (7.45 km, 30 min), climbing 1,279 meters from the city to the summit.
Glass Skywalk — 60‑meter glass‑bottomed walkway suspended on a sheer cliff face.
Heaven’s Gate (Tianmen Cave) — The world’s highest natural karst arch (131.5 m high, 57 m wide). Descend 999 steps — a sacred number in Chinese culture.
Tujia Folk Customs Park — See traditional Tujia stilt houses, learn about the 800‑year Tusi chieftain system, and admire Tujia brocade weaving.
Dayong Ancient City — Stroll through historic streets with ancient walls and traditional shops.
Optional Evening — “Tianmen Fox Fairy” (CNY 338, extra cost) — a spectacular outdoor musical set against Tianmen Mountain.

Morning: After breakfast, visit Tianmen Mountain Scenic Area (admission included). Take the round‑trip cable car to the summit. During the 30‑minute ascent, view the famous 99‑bend Tongtian Avenue winding below. At the top, follow the West Line – walk the Glass Skywalk (shoe cover included), then the cliff‑hugging Guigu Plank Road. Visit Tianmen Temple. Take the summit cable car to Yunmeng Pavilion for panoramic views. Then switch to the East Line, walk through Tong Garden, and take the mountain‑penetrating escalator down through the mountain. You will emerge at Tianmen Cave – a massive natural arch. Descend the 999 steps (or take an alternative escalator) to the base. Finally, cable car back to the city.

Lunch is included at a local restaurant.

Afternoon: Visit Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi City). Explore traditional stilted houses and learn about the Tusi chieftain system that ruled this region for over 800 years. See exhibits of Tujia brocade (xilan kapu), an intangible cultural heritage. Then stroll through Dayong Ancient City – a restored historic district with old city gates, walls, and traditional shops.

Evening: After dinner (own arrangement), you may choose to watch the outdoor musical show “Tianmen Fox Fairy” (CNY 338, optional extra). Alternatively, rest at your hotel or explore the city on your own.

Day 03
Day 3
Yuanjiajie (Avatar Mountain) & Tianzi Mountain
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Wulingyuan Scenic Area → Bailong Elevator (up) → Yuanjiajie Scenic Spot (Avatar Mountain) → First Bridge Under Heaven, Hallelujah Mountain, Back Garden, Fascination Terrace → Lunch → Tianzi Mountain (He Long Park, West Sea) → Tianzi Mountain cable car (down) → Ten‑mile Gallery (optional tram) → Return to Wulingyuan hotel → Evening optional show: “Charming Western Hunan”
✈ TransportPrivate car, eco-bus, cable car, elevator
🏨 HotelZhuanjiacun Hotel in Wulingyuan
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Bailong Elevator — The world’s tallest outdoor elevator (326 m), rising in just 66 seconds.
Yuanjiajie (Avatar Mountain) — The real‑life inspiration for Avatar’s floating mountains. See Hallelujah Mountain (Southern Sky Column) and cross the First Bridge Under Heaven — a natural stone arch spanning a 350‑meter drop.
Tianzi Mountain — “King of Peak Forests.” Enjoy endless views of stone pillars from He Long Park and West Sea.
Ten‑mile Gallery — Optional tram ride (one way CNY 38, extra) to see rock formations like “Old Man Collecting Herbs” and “Sisters Peak.”
Evening Option — “Charming Western Hunan” show (CNY 228, extra) — a famous cultural performance with Tujia and Miao songs, dances, and stunts.

Morning: After breakfast, enter the Wulingyuan Scenic Area (admission included). Take the Bailong Elevator (up) – a 326‑m glass elevator built into a cliff. In 66 seconds you are on the top of Yuanjiajie. Yuanjiajie is a natural platform mountain surrounded by higher peaks. It is famous as Avatar Mountain because the movie Avatar was filmed here. Walk along the trails to see Hallelujah Mountain (the Southern Sky Column) that directly inspired the floating mountains. Also visit the First Bridge Under Heaven – a natural rock arch connecting two peaks with a sheer drop of 350 meters. Continue to Back Garden and Fascination Terrace for more stunning views.

Lunch is served at a local restaurant (included).

Afternoon: Take a 40‑minute eco‑bus ride to He Long Park on Tianzi Mountain. Tianzi Mountain is known for its four natural spectacles: seas of clouds, sunrise, moonlit nights, and snowy peaks. From the park, enjoy magnificent scenery of countless stone pillars. Then take the Tianzi Mountain cable car down. Afterwards, visit Ten‑mile Gallery in the Suoxiyu Valley. You may take an optional tram (one way CNY 38, not included) to admire rock formations such as “Old Man Collecting Herbs,” “Sisters Peak,” and “Fingers of the Buddha.” Return to your hotel in Wulingyuan.

Evening: If you wish, your guide can book tickets for the famous show “Charming Western Hunan” (CNY 228, extra). The show features Tujia and Miao ethnic songs, dances, and spectacular stunts, providing a wonderful insight into local culture.

Day 04
Day 4
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge & Baofeng Lake – Departure
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon → Glass Bridge → (Note: this itinerary includes Baofeng Lake instead of the canyon hiking trail) → Baofeng Lake → Boat ride with Tujia folk songs → Lunch → Transfer to Zhangjiajie airport or railway station → End of tour
✈ TransportPrivate car, boat
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge — Walk on the world’s longest and highest glass‑bottomed bridge (430 m long, 300 m above the canyon floor).
Baofeng Lake — Known as the “Jade Pool of the World.” Take a boat ride on crystal‑clear water surrounded by waterfalls and unique peaks, and enjoy live Tujia ethnic folk songs performed by your guide/singer on the boat.

Morning: After breakfast, go to Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon and walk across the famous Glass Bridge – it spans 430 meters and is the longest and highest glass‑bottomed bridge in the world. The transparent floor gives you a thrilling view of the canyon floor far below.

After the bridge, instead of hiking the full canyon, this tour then takes you to Baofeng Lake (included). Baofeng Lake is a scenic alpine reservoir praised as the “Jade Pool of the World.” You will take a boat ride on the emerald lake, passing by hanging waterfalls and strange karst peaks. During the cruise, your boat driver or a local Tujia singer will perform traditional folk songs – a unique cultural experience. You are also welcome to sing along or learn a few phrases in the Tujia language.

Lunch is included at a local restaurant.

Afternoon: After the tour, your guide will transfer you to Zhangjiajie Hehua International Airport or Zhangjiajie Railway Station for your departure to your next destination. Your 4‑day Zhangjiajie tour ends here.
