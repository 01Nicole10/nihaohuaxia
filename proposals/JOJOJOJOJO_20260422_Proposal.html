Day 2
Zhangjiajie (Tianmen Mountain-Tujia Folk Customs Park)

🗺Hotel → Tianmen Mountain (cable car) → Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi Town) → Dayong Ancient City

Tianmen Mountain — Tianmen Cave, Tongtian Avenue, Ghost Plank Road, Glass Sky-walk, Tianmen Temple

After breakfast at the hotel, you’ll visit Tianmen Mountain via round-trip cable car, exploring its six wonders including the iconic Tianmen Cave and Glass Sky-walk. After lunch, you’ll experience Tujia ethnic culture at Tujia Folk Customs Park (also known as Tusi Town). In the evening, an optional performance of 'Tianmen Fox Fairy' is available.

Day 3
Zhangjiajie (Yuanjiajie & Tianzi Mountain)
▾
🗺Hotel → Bailong Elevator → Yuanjiajie Scenic Spot → He Long Park → Tianzi Mountain → Ten-mile Gallery (optional tram)
✈ TransportPrivate car, Elevator, Cable car, Tram (optional)
🏨 HotelZhuanjiacun Hotel or similar
🍽 MealsBreakfast
Yuanjiajie — Avatar Mountain, Hallelujah Mountain, No.1 Bridge in the world
Tianzi Mountain — clouds of mist, sunrise views, moonlit nights, snowcapped peaks
Ten-mile Gallery — scenic tram ride through Suoxiyu Scenic Spot

You’ll ascend to Yuanjiajie Scenic Spot via the Bailong Elevator, known as Avatar Mountain due to its filming location for the movie Avatar. Highlights include Hallelujah Mountain and the world’s highest natural quartz-sandstone bridge. After lunch, you’ll travel to He Long Park and Tianzi Mountain, famed for its four natural spectacles and over 80 viewing platforms. The day concludes with an optional tram ride along Ten-mile Gallery in Suoxiyu Scenic Spot.

Day 04
Day 4
Zhangjiajie (Bofeng Lake, Glass Bridge) - Departure
▾
🗺Hotel → Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon (Glass Bridge) → Baofeng Lake → Zhangjiajie airport or railway station
✈ TransportPrivate car
🍽 MealsBreakfast
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge — over 430 meters, longest bottom glass bridge in the world
Baofeng Lake — ‘Jade Pool in the world’, mountain waterfalls, crystal waters, Tujia folk songs on boat ride

After breakfast, you’ll visit the world-famous Glass Bridge in Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon — the longest bottom glass bridge globally, spanning over 430 meters. Next, you’ll explore Baofeng Lake, celebrated as the ‘Jade Pool in the world’, where you’ll enjoy scenic boat rides amid unique sandstone peaks and hear traditional Tujia folk songs. Finally, you’ll be transferred to Zhangjiajie airport or railway station for departure.
