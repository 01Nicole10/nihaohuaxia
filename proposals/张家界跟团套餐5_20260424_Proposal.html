Day 02
Day 2
Zhangjiajie National Forest Park – Huangshizhai & Golden Whip Stream
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Zhangjiajie National Forest Park → Round‑trip cable car up Huangshizhai → Explore six wonders of Huangshizhai → Cable car down → Lunch → Hike Golden Whip Stream (7.5 km) → Eco‑bus back → Check into Zhuanjiacun Hotel in Wulingyuan
✈ TransportPrivate car, cable car, eco‑bus
🏨 HotelZhuanjiacun Hotel
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Huangshizhai (Yellow Stone Village) — Ride the cable car (round‑trip included) to the 1,200‑meter summit. On clear days you see far into the distance; on misty days the pillars float in a sea of clouds.
Golden Whip Stream — A gentle 7.5 km trail following a crystal‑clear stream. See Golden Whip Rock, Divine Eagle Protecting the Whip, and many other poetic rock formations.

Morning: After breakfast, drive to Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (admission included). Take the round‑trip cable car to Huangshizhai (Yellow Stone Village). This peak offers the six wonders of the park: famous peaks, strange rocks, sea of clouds, winter snow, ancient trees, and grand views. Walk the flat summit loop, visiting Liuqi Pavilion, Star Picking Platform, Five Fingers Peak, and more. Descend by cable car and have lunch.

Afternoon: Hike along Golden Whip Stream, praised as the “most beautiful canyon in the world.” The 7.5 km trail is mostly flat, suitable for all fitness levels. You will pass fascinating rock formations: Golden Whip Rock, Divine Eagle Protecting the Whip, Two Turtles Exploring the Stream, and many more. The trail ends at Water Flowing Around Four Gates, where you take an eco‑bus to Wulingyuan. Check into Zhuanjiacun Hotel for the following two nights.

Evening: Free time to rest or explore the nearby area.

Day 03
Day 3
Yuanjiajie (Avatar Mountain) & Tianzi Mountain
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Wulingyuan Scenic Area → Bailong Elevator (up) → Yuanjiajie Scenic Spot (Avatar Mountain) → First Bridge Under Heaven, Hallelujah Mountain, Back Garden, Fascination Terrace → Lunch → Tianzi Mountain (He Long Park, West Sea) → Tianzi Mountain cable car (down) → Ten‑mile Gallery (optional tram) → Return to Zhuanjiacun Hotel → Optional evening show: “Charming Western Hunan”
✈ TransportPrivate car, Bailong Elevator, eco‑bus, Tianzi Mountain cable car, optional tram
🏨 HotelZhuanjiacun Hotel
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Bailong Elevator — World’s tallest outdoor elevator (326 m) rising in just 66 seconds.
Yuanjiajie (Avatar Mountain) — The real‑life inspiration for Avatar’s floating mountains. See Hallelujah Mountain (Southern Sky Column) and cross the First Bridge Under Heaven (natural stone arch with a 350‑meter drop).
Tianzi Mountain — Known for its four natural spectacles: clouds, sunrise, moonlight, and snowy peaks. Over 80 platforms offer a “sea of pillars” panorama.
Ten‑mile Gallery — Optional tram ride (CNY 38 one way, not included) to view rock formations like “Old Man Collecting Herbs.”
Evening Option — “Charming Western Hunan” show (CNY 228, extra), a spectacular ethnic cultural performance.

Morning: After breakfast, re‑enter the Wulingyuan Scenic Area (admission included). Take the Bailong Elevator up to Yuanjiajie (Avatar Mountain). Walk the trails to see the First Bridge Under Heaven – a natural rock arch connecting two peaks with a dramatic drop. Stand before Hallelujah Mountain (the Southern Sky Column), the pillar that directly inspired the floating mountains in Avatar. Continue to Back Garden and Fascination Terrace for more stunning views of pillars rising out of green valleys.

Lunch is served at a local restaurant.

Afternoon: Take a 40‑minute eco‑bus ride to He Long Park on Tianzi Mountain. Tianzi Mountain is called the “King of Peak Forests.” Your guide will lead you to the best viewpoints – West Sea, Imperial Writing Brush Peak, Fairy Presenting Flowers – offering endless vistas of stone pillars. Then take the Tianzi Mountain cable car down. Afterwards, visit Ten‑mile Gallery in the Suoxiyu Valley. You may walk or take an optional tram (CNY 38 one way, not included) to see rock formations such as “Old Man Collecting Herbs” and “Sisters Peak.” Return to your hotel.

Evening: You may choose to watch the famous “Charming Western Hunan” show (CNY 228, optional extra), which features Tujia and Miao songs, dances, and stunts.

Day 04
Day 4
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge & Baofeng Lake
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon → Glass Bridge crossing → (Note: This package includes Baofeng Lake instead of full canyon hike) → Baofeng Lake → Boat ride with Tujia folk songs → Lunch → Return to Zhuanjiacun Hotel
✈ TransportPrivate car, boat
🏨 HotelZhuanjiacun Hotel
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge — Walk on the world’s longest and highest glass‑bottomed bridge (430 m long, 300 m high).
Baofeng Lake — Cruise the “Jade Pool of the World” on a scenic boat ride. Hear traditional Tujia folk songs performed by local singers on the lake.

Morning: After breakfast, go to Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon and walk across the famous Glass Bridge – over 430 meters long, it is the longest and highest glass‑bottomed bridge in the world. The transparent floor gives a thrilling view of the canyon floor far below. (This tour does not include the full canyon hike; it focuses on the bridge and then proceeds to Baofeng Lake.)

After the bridge, visit Baofeng Lake, which is praised as the “Jade Pool of the World.” Take a boat ride on the crystal‑clear emerald lake, surrounded by hanging waterfalls, unique karst peaks, and lush vegetation. On the boat, a local Tujia singer will perform traditional folk songs, and you can learn a few words or sing along.

Lunch is included at a local restaurant.

Afternoon: Return to your hotel in Wulingyuan. The rest of the day is free for you to rest or explore.

Day 05
Day 5
Tianmen Mountain, Tujia Folk Customs Park & Departure
▾
🗺Hotel breakfast → Check‑out → Tianmen Mountain Scenic Area (cable car up) → West Line: Glass Skywalk, Guigu Plank Road, Tianmen Temple → Summit cable car → Yunmeng Pavilion → East Line → Mountain‑penetrating escalator → Tianmen Cave → Descend 999 steps → Cable car down → Lunch → Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi City) → Dayong Ancient City → Transfer to Zhangjiajie airport/railway station → End of tour
✈ TransportPrivate car, cable car, mountain‑penetrating escalator, cable car
🍽 MealsBreakfast, Lunch
Tianmen Mountain — The “Soul of Zhangjiajie.” Ride the world’s longest cable car (7.45 km, 30 min) to the summit. Walk the Glass Skywalk, the frightening Guigu Plank Road, and pass through Heaven’s Gate (a natural arch 131.5 m high) before descending the legendary 999 steps.
Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi City) — Explore traditional Tujia stilt houses, learn about the 800‑year Tusi chieftain system, and see Tujia brocade weaving.

Morning: After breakfast and hotel check‑out, visit Tianmen Mountain Scenic Area (admission included). Take the round‑trip cable car – one of the world’s longest – from the city to the summit. During the 30‑minute ascent, view the famous 99‑bend Tongtian Avenue winding below. At the top, follow the West Line: walk the Glass Skywalk (shoe cover included), the cliff‑hugging Guigu Plank Road, and visit Tianmen Temple. Then take the summit cable car to Yunmeng Pavilion for panoramic views. Switch to the East Line, walk through Tong Garden, and take the mountain‑penetrating escalator down to Tianmen Cave (Heaven’s Gate). Descend the 999 steps (or take an alternative escalator) to the base. Finally, cable car back to the city.

Lunch is included at a local restaurant.

Afternoon: Visit Tujia Folk Customs Park (Tusi City). Here you will see traditional Tujia stilted houses and learn about the Tusi chieftain system that lasted over 800 years. Admire Tujia brocade (xilan kapu), a recognized intangible cultural heritage. Then stroll through Dayong Ancient City, a restored historic district with ancient city walls, gates, and traditional shops.

After the tour, your guide will transfer you to Zhangjiajie Hehua International Airport or Zhangjiajie Railway Station for your departure. Your 5‑day Zhangjiajie tour ends here.
