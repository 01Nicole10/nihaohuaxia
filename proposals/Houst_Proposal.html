A Journey from Lingnan Culture to Avatar Mountains and Sichuan Treasures

Day by Day
Your Detailed Itinerary
Day 01
June 5
Guangzhou
▾
✈ TransportPrivate transfer
Hong Kong airport pick-up, transfer to Guangzhou hotel, rest

Welcome to China! After your long journey from Houston via Vancouver, your guide will meet you at Hong Kong International Airport and escort you across the border to Guangzhou. Today is dedicated to rest and recovery, allowing you to adjust to the local time.

Day 02
June 6
Guangzhou
▾
🍽 MealsHotel breakfast
Chen Clan Academy, Yuexiu Park, Shamian Island, Yongqingfang, Canton Tower (exterior), Pearl River Night Cruise

Begin at Chen Clan Academy, a magnificent complex representing the pinnacle of Lingnan artistry. Next, visit Yuexiu Park, a sprawling green oasis. Explore Shamian Island, a sandbar that became a British and French concession after the Second Opium War (1861). Continue to Yongqingfang on Enning Road, Guangzhou’s longest-preserved arcade street built in 1931. As dusk falls, view the Canton Tower from the riverside before boarding your Pearl River night cruise for spectacular views of the illuminated skyline.

Day 03
June 7
Guangzhou → Zhangjiajie
▾
✈ TransportHigh-speed train (6–7 hours)
🍽 MealsHotel breakfast
High-speed train to Zhangjiajie, station pick-up, hotel check-in

After breakfast, transfer to Guangzhou South Railway Station for a high-speed train to Zhangjiajie. China‘s high-speed rail network is renowned for its punctuality and comfort. As you travel north, watch the landscape transform from the Pearl River Delta’s dense urban fabric to the lush, karst-studded hills of Hunan province.

Day 04
June 8
Zhangjiajie
▾
Yuanjiajie, Avatar Hallelujah Mountain, Bailong Elevator, Ten-Mile Gallery

Walk through the real-life inspiration for the Hallelujah Mountains from James Cameron’s Avatar. The towering quartz-sandstone pillars of Yuanjiajie, including the famous Hallelujah Mountain (Southern Sky Column), create an otherworldly landscape. Ride the Bailong Elevator, the world‘s tallest outdoor elevator built into a cliff face, and explore the dramatic Ten-Mile Gallery.

Day 05
June 9
Zhangjiajie → Chongqing
▾
✈ TransportCable car, train
Tianmen Mountain (Glass Skywalk, Heaven‘s Gate), Tujia Folk Customs Park, train to Chongqing

Experience one of the world‘s longest cable car rides (30 minutes) from the city center to the summit. Challenge your courage on the glass-bottomed skywalk suspended on a sheer cliff face, then witness Heaven’s Gate—a massive natural arch 131.5 meters high—and descend the legendary 999 steps.

Day 06
June 10
Chongqing
▾
Jiefangbei, Bayi Food Street, Kuixinglou, Hongyadong night view

Discover China‘s most unique urban landscape. Witness the magical transformation of Hongyadong, a complex of traditional stilted houses that blazes with golden lights after dark. Sample authentic Chongqing hot pot and local snacks on Bayi Food Street, visit the iconic Jiefangbei monument, and explore the ancient Ciqikou Town.

Day 07
June 11
Chongqing → Chengdu
▾
✈ TransportTrain
Liziba Light Rail Station, Eling No.2 Factory, Ciqikou Ancient Town, train to Chengdu
Day 08
June 12
Chengdu
▾
Chengdu Panda Base, Sanxingdui Museum

Get up close with giant pandas at the Chengdu Panda Base—arrive early to see them most active. Journey back 3,000 years at Sanxingdui Museum to discover the mysterious bronze masks of the ancient Shu kingdom.

Day 09
June 13
Chengdu
▾
Wuhou Shrine, People’s Park, Chunxi Road

Experience Chengdu‘s famous teahouse culture at People’s Park and explore the vibrant Jinli Ancient Street near Wuhou Shrine.
