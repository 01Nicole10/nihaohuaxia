Day 2 | May 23
Mutianyu Great Wall + Bird’s Nest
▾
🗺Hotel → Mutianyu Great Wall → Bird’s Nest & Water Cube (exterior) → Hotel
✈ TransportPrivate car
🍽 MealsB
Mutianyu Great Wall — less crowded, well-preserved Ming Dynasty section with panoramic mountain views
Bird’s Nest and Water Cube — iconic Olympic venues (exterior photos only)

Day 3 | May 24
Forbidden City → Xi’an
▾
🗺Hotel → Forbidden City → High-speed rail station → Xi’an
✈ TransportHigh-speed train
🍽 MealsB
Forbidden City (Palace Museum) — largest ancient royal palace in China
Ever-Bright Tang City — immersive Tang dynasty-themed night stroll

Enjoy a full-day guided tour of the Forbidden City, exploring its imperial history and architectural grandeur. In the afternoon, take a high-speed rail to Xi’an, followed by free time to explore Ever-Bright Tang City in the evening.

Day 04
Day 4 | May 25
Terracotta Army + Pottery DIY
▾
🗺Hotel → Terracotta Army Museum → Pottery DIY workshop → Muslim Street
✈ TransportPrivate car
🍽 MealsB
Terracotta Army Museum — world-renowned archaeological wonder
Pottery figurine DIY workshop — hands-on experience of ancient Qin craftsmanship
Muslim Street — historic food block with authentic Xi’an snacks and folk shops

Visit the Terracotta Army Museum to witness the extraordinary life-sized clay warriors. Participate in a pottery DIY workshop to connect with ancient Qin techniques. Later, wander through Muslim Street to taste local delicacies and soak up the vibrant night market atmosphere.

Day 05
Day 5 | May 26
Xi’an City Wall Cycling → Zhangjiajie
▾
🗺Hotel → Xi’an Ancient City Wall (cycling) → High-speed rail station → Zhangjiajie
✈ TransportHigh-speed train
🍽 MealsB
Xi’an Ancient City Wall — well-preserved Ming dynasty fortification, ideal for cycling
High-speed rail transfer to Zhangjiajie

Begin the day with an invigorating bike ride along the top of Xi’an’s ancient city wall, offering great views and photo opportunities. In the afternoon, board a high-speed train bound for Zhangjiajie, arriving in time for rest and preparation for mountain adventures.

Day 06
Day 6 | May 27
Yuanjiajie / Yangjiajie / Sky Pasture / Tianzishan
▾
🗺Hotel → Zhangjiajie National Forest Park → Bailong Sky Elevator → Yuanjiajie → Yangjiajie → Sky Pasture shuttle → Tianzishan Cable Car → Exit
✈ TransportPrivate car, shuttle bus, cable car
🍽 MealsB
Yuanjiajie — filming location of Avatar, famed for quartz-sandstone pillars
Yangjiajie — unique peak-wall landforms, 2—3 hour leisure hike
Sky Pasture — scenic plateau accessible by shuttle bus
Tianzishan Cable Car — descent option with weight limit ≤ 120kg

Enter Zhangjiajie National Forest Park and ascend via the Bailong Sky Elevator to Yuanjiajie, home to the iconic 'Avatar Hallelujah Mountain'. Then enjoy a relaxed hike in Yangjiajie, known for its dramatic rock formations. Continue with a shuttle to Sky Pasture before descending via the Tianzishan Cable Car.

Day 07
Day 7 | May 28
Golden Whip Stream Hiking + Huangshizhai
▾
🗺Hotel → Golden Whip Stream → Huangshizhai Cable Car → Huangshizhai summit → downhill walk
✈ TransportCable car, walking
🍽 MealsB
Golden Whip Stream — peaceful forest valley with clear streams, 2—3 hours walking
Huangshizhai — mountaintop panoramic views of peak forest, accessed by one-way cable car
Gentle downhill walk — ~1—1.5 hours

Start with a tranquil 2–3 hour hike along Golden Whip Stream, winding through lush forest and past crystal-clear waters. Ride the one-way cable car up to Huangshizhai for sweeping vistas of Zhangjiajie’s signature sandstone peaks. Conclude with a gentle downhill walk back to the base.

Day 08
Day 8 | May 29
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon + Huanglong Cave
▾
🗺Hotel → Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon & Glass Bridge → Zip Line → Slideway → Huanglong Cave
✈ TransportPrivate car, zip line, slideway
🍽 MealsB
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge — world’s highest and longest glass-bottomed bridge
Zip line + slideway — fully arranged descent to valley bottom, no extra walking
Huanglong Cave — vast karst cave with underground river and stalactite formations

Explore the awe-inspiring Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon, crossing the famous Glass Bridge. Experience the adrenaline rush of the zip line and slideway combination for a seamless descent to the valley floor. Later, tour Huanglong Cave to marvel at its subterranean rivers, chambers, and geological wonders.

Day 09
Day 9 | May 30
Tianmenshan Full Day Tour
▾
🗺Hotel → Tianmenshan Mountain (alpine cable car) → Cliff plank road → Glass walkway → Tianmen Cave → Mountain temple
✈ TransportCable car
🍽 MealsB
Tianmenshan Alpine Cable Car — one of the world’s longest and highest cable cars
Cliff plank road and glass walkway — thrilling cliffside pathways
Tianmen Cave — natural arch and sacred site

Ascend Tianmenshan Mountain via the spectacular alpine cable car. Walk the vertiginous cliff plank road and glass walkway for unforgettable mountain views. Visit the majestic Tianmen Cave — a massive natural arch — and explore the serene mountain temple nestled in the peaks.
